Curious Dog

Dogs are curious creatures by nature and that curiosity can get your pooch into trouble from time to time - or all of the time! There are so many sights, sounds, and smell to explore.

  • Body size: Many shapes and sizes.
  • Metabolism: Higher metabolism than people.
  • Temperature regulation: Much better at conserving heat than at cooling themselves.
  • Senses: Same 5 senses that people do.
